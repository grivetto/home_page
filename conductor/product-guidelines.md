# Product Guidelines

## Content Tone and Voice
- **Professional and Trustworthy:** The content will focus on expertise, clarity, and reliability to build confidence in the personal brand.

## Visual Identity
- **Clean and Modern Design:** Prioritize white space, clear typography, and a streamlined layout.
- **Light Blue Palette:** The primary color scheme will utilize various shades of light blue to create a professional and serene atmosphere.

## Typography
- **Modern Sans-Serif:** Use sans-serif fonts (like Inter or Roboto) for all content to ensure a modern, accessible, and highly readable experience across all devices.

## Animation and Interaction
- **Dynamic and Immersive:** Incorporate scroll-based animations and smooth transitions to create an engaging and interactive user experience.
